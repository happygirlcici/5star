RESPONSIVE & MOBILE WEBSITES

Responsive web design is an approach to web design aimed at allowing desktop webpages to be viewed in response to the size of the screen or web browser one is viewing with.

Why is this important?

In 2014 there were nearly 1 billion personal computers world-wide, with estimates for that number to grow to 2 billion by the end of 2016.

That being said, it is estimated that tablet users will surpass 1.2 billion users world-wide and the amount of cellphone surfers will surpass 2 billion users by the end of 2016. These figures are expected to continue to grow, and we will help you be ready for all these smart device users.
